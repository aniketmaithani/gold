The Intent

G.O.L.D's intent is to grow into a sporting movement in the years to come. To travel across India, identify and inspire potential sporting talent, to stand tall and be counted in the sporting hall of fame and indent the world sport map, beyond cricket and hockey.

We are confident that with the growth of sporting patriotism and co-dependence, we'll soon be invited by the Ministry of Sports at the State level to bring G.O.L.D to every state to assist in discovering new talent.

We may grow from supporting 20 athletes representing Indian Sport at a national level, to be able to support and groom 20 athletes per state to represent India in different sporting disciplines.

Yet, every dream begins with the FIRST IRREVERSIBLE STEP FORWARD...

How We Make It Happen

I

LEVEL I: School Identification

Intra-school identification of 3 athletes per school based on their race timing at the local school level. Inter-school race day where representatives from each school compete to reveal the top 20 runners at the state level.

II

LEVEL II: Three-Day Development Camp

20 potential athletes participate in a 3-day camp with their parents/school representatives. Introduction to Nutrition Experts, Fitness Experts, and Academic Experts who will assist parents and schools in understanding their role in supporting the child and planning comprehensive support schedules.

III

LEVEL III: Commitment & Training

Due diligence of signing contracts until their first representation and winning performance. A 3-month fitness/nutrition/academic schedule is established with ongoing monitoring and support.

IV

LEVEL IV: Update and Upgrade

Based on performance of the 3-month regime and preparedness in a 1-day camp, we introduce discipline-specific coaching and mentoring schedule, including interactions with eminent sporting personalities who share the pride and honor of representing Indian sport.

V

LEVEL V: National Introduction

Introduce this new talent to India in a showcase run and formally present them at a special event. Continue to find avenues and races for them to train at both nationally and internationally.

Financing & Partnership

Level I Funding

Seeking a national brand to sponsor the School Contact Programme to initiate the search. This level requires the most critical attention to detail. Open to brand partnership through the entire first batch journey.

Level II Support

Co-dependence with existing brands in fitness, nutrition, and academics to sponsor students' basic needs. Creating healthy competition among brands to produce results and grow in mass popularity as contributing brands.

G.I.V.E Basis

Open to barter deals where brands sponsor children's fitness/nutrition/academic needs, embodying their brand vision through the talent they support. Citizen fundraising activation for Indians to donate to G.O.L.D.

Level III & IV Investment

Creating media-rich introduction of the 20 talents, brands, coaches, and mentors. Giving corporate and business India the opportunity to unite as part of the movement by co-sponsoring talented children. Multiple brand support and extensive media plans with fundraising events.
